Privacy Policy

Effective June 11, 2026

Dopo helps you save posts and videos you find on social platforms into one searchable library. This page explains what information Dopo collects, how it's used, and the choices you have. We've kept it in plain English on purpose.

What we collect

  • Account information. Your email address and a password (stored as a secure hash — we can't read it).
  • Your saves. The links you save and their public metadata: title, creator name, thumbnail, platform, and similar details. Dopo saves links, not media files — we don't download or host the videos themselves.
  • Your organization. Folders (collections) you create, items you favorite, notes and tags you add, and the people you share folders with.
  • Notifications. If you allow push notifications, we store a device token so Apple can deliver them to your phone.
  • Basic logs. Standard technical logs (like app version and error reports) that help us keep the service working.

How we use it

  • To run Dopo: storing your library, powering search, syncing folders you share with others, and sending notifications about activity in shared folders.
  • AI organization: the public metadata of links you save is processed by AI services (such as Google Gemini or OpenAI) to generate tags, summaries, and search results. Your password and private account details are never sent to these services.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell your personal information.
  • We don't show third-party ads or share your data with advertisers.
  • We don't post anything to your social accounts — Dopo never has access to them.

Who else touches the data

Dopo runs on trusted infrastructure providers that process data on our behalf: Supabase (database and sign-in), Vercel (web hosting), Apple (push notification delivery), and AI providers for tagging as described above. We may also disclose information if the law requires it.

Sharing inside Dopo

If you add a save to a shared folder, the people in that folder can see that save and your account name. Folders you make public via a share link can be seen by anyone with the link. Everything else in your library is private to you.

Your choices

  • Delete any save or folder at any time inside the app.
  • Turn off push notifications in iOS Settings whenever you like.
  • To delete your account and all data tied to it, email admin@legacyaisolutions.ai and we'll take care of it promptly.

Children

Dopo isn't directed at children under 13, and we don't knowingly collect their information.

Changes

If this policy changes in a meaningful way, we'll update this page and the effective date above.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email admin@legacyaisolutions.ai.
